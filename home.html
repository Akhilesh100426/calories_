What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure that uses your height and weight to estimate if you have a healthy body weight. It helps in assessing potential health risks related to underweight, overweight, or obesity.

Why is BMI Important?

Maintaining a healthy BMI reduces the risk of diseases such as heart disease, diabetes, and hypertension. It helps in monitoring weight-related health concerns effectively.

Calories and Nutrition

Caloric intake plays a crucial role in maintaining a balanced diet. Consuming the right amount of calories helps in achieving fitness goals, whether it is weight loss, maintenance, or muscle gain.
